# THE VAULTED SKY — film treatment (first pass)

Adapted from the Mewtwo interludes of *Pokemon: The Origin of Species* by DaystarEld
(chapters 28, 40, 59, 75, 81–83, 85 — full beat sheets with verbatim quotes in `notes/`,
extracted chapter text in `source/`).

Target: ~52 shots × ~8s ≈ 8–9 minutes. 480p fast draft → std master, Seedance 2.0 +
Nano Banana anchors/frames, per `MOVIE_LESSONS.md`.

**Canon corrections vs. the director's memory** (all sourced, see notes/):
- There is **no volcanic eruption**. The escape happens during the *aftermath* of the
  Groudon/Kyogre cataclysm — region-wide earthquakes + supernatural storm wreck the lab;
  Mewtwo bolts at sunset, the moment word arrives that Groudon is beaten and *Sabrina is
  teleporting back*. (A volcano IS visible at the island's heart — we can keep it looming
  in frame as atmosphere.)
- The on-page game with Giovanni is **Go** (he also taught Mewtwo chess, among others —
  "until I mastered him in each"). Go's orbiting black/white stones are the better visual.
- The big telekinetic blast at combat trials is triggered by **wounded pride** ("average")
  *before* the rattata bite; the bite instead nearly triggers his secret **self-healing**,
  which he suppresses by stomping his own wounded foot.
- The tail is bitten off by an **unnamed Dark predator** mid-ocean (sharpedo is only
  foreshadowed elsewhere — staging it AS a sharpedo is fair game).
- The minders ("comforters") do know their job — broadcast calm near "the subject" — but
  have never seen him. Eva reads poetry aloud; Gyokusho draws; Megan meditates.

## Visual style bible

One cinematic language, three worlds:

| World | Palette / look |
|---|---|
| The pod (Acts 1–2) | Amber bio-fluid glow in deep blue-black lab dark; EKG beep as metronome; layers of glass everywhere; faces lit by monitor light |
| The lab & mansion (Acts 3–5) | Clinical white/grey corridors funneling upward into warm stone mansion halls; then the shock of full color — azure/emerald/pearl clifftop, sea light |
| The cataclysm & freedom (Acts 6–8) | Rain, red emergency light, dusk gold; then moonless night — endless stars, black ocean, bioluminescent blues |

Recurring motifs (keep in prompts):
- **Glass between faces** — pod glass, helmet visor, observation windows. The film's whole
  arc is layers of glass coming off.
- **The EKG beep** — womb rhythm in Act 1, sedation, the suit's low-potion warning, gone
  forever after the escape.
- **Hands** — Fuji's sticky note; Mewtwo's fist-then-open-palm on the glass; Sabrina's five
  tan fingers wrapped around his three white ones. Final echo: his hand clenching wet sand.
- **Circles/orbits** — the ring of comforter rooms, Go stones orbiting the tube, the ring
  of guards, the unown array.
- **The sky** — borrowed sun-memory → "the vaulted sky" clifftop → the star-flight.

## Cast (anchors — generate FIRST, reuse as `--image` refs everywhere)

- **mewtwo_pod** — Mewtwo in the biopod: sickly bone-white skin, humanoid but alien;
  pinched face, no lips visible, no eyebrows, short snout; violet eyes; two blunt horns;
  three thick bulbous fingers per hand; long purple-grey tail; digitigrade legs; curled
  in glowing amber fluid, tubes running into arms/legs/torso from above and below.
  Face nearly expressionless — "slight curves of its lips" only.
- **mewtwo_suit1** — same creature in Suit v1: bulky rounded metal tubes, back battery,
  full helmet with round glass visor. Clumsy, humiliating.
- **mewtwo_armor** — Suit v2/v3: angular dark-grey armor plates, partial helmet with two
  grooves for the horns, glass visor, exposed tail and thighs. Imposing, sinister — armor
  more than suit. (This is the escape-era look.)
- **mewtwo_free** — no suit at all: lean, scarred, weathered white-purple body, tail
  MISSING (stump) for the final shots only.
- **sabrina_teen** — Sabrina at ~17: long dark hair, pale, solemn, plain dark clothing;
  a faint violet aura only Mewtwo sees (use sparingly, in his POV shots).
- **sabrina** — Sabrina at ~27: Saffron Gym Leader; long dark hair like a curtain,
  composed, quietly warm; simple elegant dark clothes.
- **giovanni** — tall, strong shoulders, neat black suit, tan skin, strong jaw,
  close-cropped dark hair; calm face, intense dark eyes; handkerchief in pocket;
  movements "quick and purposeful without seeming hurried."
- **fuji** — Dr. Fuji: old, kind, grief-lined face, white hair, glasses, rumpled lab coat.
- **dr_light** — Dr. Ivy Light, lab director: 50s, sharp practical face, short hair,
  lab coat over field clothes; exhausted competence.
- **shaw** — David Shaw, head of security: ex-cop build, weathered, buzz cut, dark
  tactical clothing; plus his **weavile** (red feather crest, gold gem) at heel.
- **gyokusho** — Kawabata Gyokusho, young comforter-artist: messy dark hair, gentle,
  nervous; sketchbook.
- **eva** — Eva, middle-aged comforter: soft cardigan, reading glasses, poetry book.
- **ayush** — Ayush, suit engineer/doctor: 30s, bright-eyed, enthusiastic, toolkit vest.
- **collins** — Dr. Perry Collins: mid-40s scientist, thinning hair, glasses, lab coat
  (the man in the great ball).
- Guard pokemon refs: **umbreon, mightyena, absol, bisharp** (pod room);
  **weavile, greninja, hydreigon, krookodile, mandibuzz, honchkrow** (escape).

## Locations (plates)

- **loc_pod_room** — circular underground chamber: the glass biopod glowing amber at
  center, tubes to ceiling/floor, ring of monitors and speakers, keyboard on an arm,
  heartbeat monitor; dark beyond the equipment.
- **loc_comforter_room** — small warm room: armchair, lamp, bookshelf, drawings pinned
  to walls; a curving outer wall (the ring around his chamber).
- **loc_lab_corridor** — white/grey branching corridors with heavy doors; each higher
  level wider than the last (inverted funnel).
- **loc_conference** — windowless lab conference room, long table, paper logbook,
  whiteboard of project codes, tired scientists.
- **loc_holochamber** — a perfectly bare white room, edge-less.
- **loc_mansion** — ornate stone mansion interior: tiled floors, balconies, tall doors
  opening to light.
- **loc_clifftop** — grassy plateau on a cliff over the ocean, stone steps, wind; a
  volcanic peak hazy at the island's heart; the mansion behind.
- **loc_training** — stark white training room with an observation deck behind glass.
- **loc_ocean** — open ocean at dusk/night: waves, spray, dark fliers against a sunset.
- **loc_cave** — pitch-black sea cave: wet sand, still water, the suit's single blinking
  light.
- **loc_nightsky** — moonless night sky over black ocean, endless stars.

## Voice locks

- **Mewtwo (synthesized)**: deep, resonant, calm baritone through a speaker — measured,
  somber, faintly electronic. Face NEVER moves when speaking (no mouth movement — the
  voice comes from the suit/speaker).
- **Mewtwo (telepathic/inner)**: same baritone but warm and unprocessed, heard while his
  face stays completely still. The tulpas are variations: **Doubt** hissed and urgent,
  **Trust** gentle and even, **Flourish** quick and delighted, **Victory** flat and cold.
- **Sabrina**: young woman, low, calm, precise; telepathic lines heard while her lips
  don't move; never theatrical.
- **Giovanni**: deep, commanding, unhurried; the voice of a man who has never needed to
  raise it.
- **Dr. Light**: dry, crisp, professional; fatigue underneath.
- **Shaw**: gravelly, flat cop-voice.
- NO narrator anywhere; only characters on screen (or established diegetic sources —
  speakers, recordings, telepathy) speak.

## Shot list

### Act 1 — 2.351 (birth in the dark)

- **a1** — Cold open, abstract: darkness, muffled warped voices, amber light refracting
  through fluid and glass, a slow EKG beep. Fragments of borrowed images bloom and
  dissolve: a sun in a blue sky, a smiling face, "lights in the dark." (no dialogue)
- **a2** — First reveal: WIDE of loc_pod_room — the small curled creature floating in
  amber fluid, tubes in his arms and spine, monitors pulsing. MEWTWO (inner, childlike
  wonder): "Who am I? Where am I? …What am I?"
- **a3** — The minders: Eva reading poetry aloud alone to her empty room; Gyokusho
  drawing a bird in flight; Megan meditating — intercut with the pod. MEWTWO (inner):
  "Around me, always, there are minds. They give me their calm. They have never seen
  my face."
- **a4** — Teen Sabrina seated cross-legged near the pod, eyes closed. SABRINA
  (telepathic): "Be calm. My name is Sabrina. You are subject 2.351 — the first
  successful hybrid of a human and a pokemon." MEWTWO (inner, panicking): "Is this what
  death is?!" SABRINA: "Calm. Two plus two is four."
- **a5** — Fuji alone at his monitor at night, grief on his face. MEWTWO (inner,
  hesitant): "Lonely. Yes." Fuji bolts upright, stares around the empty office — then
  writes something. CU: a sticky note stuck to the monitor's edge: "You are not alone."
- **a6** — The pod cover lifts for the first time; painful bright light; blurred
  silhouettes resolve. A mirror is wheeled up to the glass. Mewtwo's violet eyes fix on
  his own reflection. MEWTWO (inner): "I am a monster." SABRINA: "I have never known a
  monster to call themselves one. You are what you choose to be."
- **a7** — Giovanni alone at the glass, hands behind his back, breath fogging it, lips
  almost touching. GIOVANNI: "Can you hear me? …Truth, then, between us. You were
  created to end death. If you survive, you will be a titan who reshapes the world."
  Mewtwo taps a fist on the glass, opens the palm. GIOVANNI: "Soon. And we will change
  it into a paradise."

### Act 2 — Ten years of lies

- **b1** — TIME CUT: same pod, Mewtwo grown to fill it. Screens ring the glass — films,
  books, a keyboard floating on telekinesis. MEWTWO (inner, cold now): "Ten years in
  this tube. Ten years of lies. My illness never improves. And these humans care
  nothing for me."
- **b2** — The censored library: pages and film clips flicking past his fixed violet
  eyes, faster and faster. MEWTWO (inner): "Thousands of books. Thousands of films. And
  in all of them — not one story of a prisoner who escapes. Whoever chooses what I see
  is afraid of what I might learn."
- **b3** — Go across the glass: Giovanni seated at a board, speaking into an amplifier;
  inside the fluid, black and white stones orbit Mewtwo's tube in twin rings. MEWTWO
  (synthesized, via speaker): "You are losing again, Giovanni." GIOVANNI (faint smile):
  "So I am." MEWTWO (inner): "But they are only games. In the only one that matters, he
  holds all the pieces."
- **b4** — Sabrina, adult now, palm against the glass. SABRINA (telepathic): "We think
  we found a way to bring you out, Mazda." CU his eyes widening; the EKG spikes; his
  face stays a mask. MEWTWO (inner): "Damn them. Damn them all — most of all for the
  hope they keep alive, like a starving flower."

### Act 3 — The suit and the sky

- **c1** — The pod drains, gurgling; he collapses on wet glass, raw and shaking;
  needles withdraw; his whole body seizes with pain, the EKG racing. SABRINA (urgent,
  telepathic): "Mazda, breathe! You have to breathe!" A gasp.
- **c2** — Suit v1 assembled around him piece by piece; sweet relief; he stands and
  turns fully around for the first time in his life; the watching humans step back;
  a guard's umbreon growls. MEWTWO (inner, trembling): "I'm free. I'm free."
- **c3** — The walk: armored Mewtwo at Eva's door, entourage crowding the corridor.
  SABRINA (aloud, for him): "Mewtwo wants to express its gratitude — for the poems."
  EVA (flustered, glowing): "Oh! You're quite welcome, Mewtwo!" He bows, tail lifting
  for balance.
- **c4** — The final staircase: he freezes at the last flight, the emptiness above.
  A slim hand wraps around his three-fingered paw. SABRINA (telepathic): "We shall go
  together." They climb into light.
- **c5** — THE SKY (key shot): mansion doors open onto the clifftop — azure and navy
  and emerald and pearl; wind; grass crushing under his feet. He grips her hand too
  hard. MEWTWO (telepathic, overwhelmed): "The sky is too big, Sabrina — it is too
  big — I will fall up into it—" Both of them are crying; tears run inside his visor.
- **c6** — The suit begins to beep. GIOVANNI (off the wind, flat): "We must go back
  now, Mewtwo." Long still shot: Mewtwo facing the open sea, the ring of guards and
  Dark pokemon waiting. MEWTWO (inner, breaking): "I don't want to die. …I am too
  weak. I return."

### Act 4 — The machine (Giovanni's world)

- **d1** — loc_conference: exhausted scientists around the long table, paper logbook.
  DR. SATO: "Nine labs. Staff for seven. Every project is 'critical'." DR. MARTIN: "Do
  you want the superweapon's room understaffed on the day it decides it wants out?"
- **d2** — The door opens. Giovanni enters, alone, unannounced. Silence like a dropped
  blade. GIOVANNI (pleasant): "Don't mind me. I won't distract you all any further —
  Dr. Collins, I just came for you. Completely unrelated." He draws a great ball from
  inside his jacket.
- **d3** — Scientists dive behind furniture; Collins bolts — "Please, I can explain—" —
  the ball strikes his back; his wail cuts off mid-scream as he's pulled inside. The
  ball rolls and clicks against a table leg. Silence. GIOVANNI: "Would you mind,
  Dr. Light?" Her trembling fingers pick it up and hand her colleague to her boss.
- **d4** — After the door shuts: the frozen room. DR. LIGHT (steady, barely): "Those in
  favor of a slight scaling down of operations… raise your hand." Every hand rises.
- **d5** — The pipeline, compressed: an execution room; a syringe set gently on a
  table; a hooded young prisoner. GIOVANNI: "There's no non-poison one. It's just a
  matter of dosage. I advise you keep still." MATCH CUT: the same prisoner, alive, at
  a ship's rail, watching an island grow on the horizon.
- **d6** — loc_holochamber: Giovanni faces "Mewtwo" in a perfect simulation of the pod
  room. SIM-MEWTWO: "Shall we play a game?" GIOVANNI: "Not today. Mewtwo — your illness
  is artificially maintained. We found a cure years ago. I kept it from you." SIM-MEWTWO
  (graceful): "Then cure me, and we can begin building true trust between us." Giovanni
  closes his eyes — and presses a button. Mewtwo vanishes; the whole room bleaches to
  bare white. Giovanni alone, dabbing sweat with a handkerchief. GIOVANNI (quiet): "I've
  lost perspective."

### Act 5 — Children of the mind

- **e1** — Autumn clifftop, the volcano hazy inland: armored Mewtwo walking with
  Sabrina and Ayush, breath of cold wind. MEWTWO (synthesized): "It is colder. I can
  feel the sun's warmth, but the air does not carry it into my bones."
- **e2** — Levitation: he rises an inch off the grass; Sabrina laughs, hair and clothes
  lifting in scintillating light, her shoes leaving the ground. SABRINA: "Don't be
  ridiculous — we flew!"
- **e3** — KEY DECEPTION SHOT: later, walking "beside" Ayush at sunset — a low angle
  reveals his feet gliding a few centimeters above the grass while invisible force
  presses perfect footprints into the turf behind him, one by one. (no dialogue; wind
  and birdsong)
- **e4** — Night. The pod, dark, sealed. His eyes closed, face still. The tulpas in the
  dark, voices circling. DOUBT (hissed): "They're not guarding you from others — they're
  guarding others from you!" TRUST (even): "We have yet to catch Sabrina in a single
  lie." FLOURISH (delighted): "A flight risk!" MEWTWO/PRIME (calm, commanding): "You
  have your orders. Safety. Then power. Then freedom — and hide my true self."
- **e5** — Rain-lashed clifftop, both soaked. SABRINA (telepathic, gentle): "You know
  you don't have to do anything you don't want to. If you're afraid — you can say so."
  Beat. MEWTWO (the scripted answer, perfect): "All this power must be used for
  something. So many people put their hopes in me. I cannot turn my back on them."
  SABRINA (smiling through the rain): "You are truly too good for us, Mazda."
- **e6** — loc_training: a kangaskhan doll slides weakly across the floor. Behind
  glass, murmurs. SABRINA (clinical): "It seems strange that the force of your kinesis
  is so… average." His fingers twitch — then a BANG: the doll slams into the far wall
  hard enough to shake the lights. (pride overriding the con)
- **e7** — The rattata: he stands motionless and lets the little pokemon leap and bite
  into his calf — real blood, real pain; he flails, bellows, stomps his own wounded
  foot. GIOVANNI (curious, through glass): "Mewtwo. Why aren't you using your
  abilities?" MEWTWO (synthesized, controlled again): "I wanted to feel it. And… I am
  scared. Of killing my opponent."
- **e8** — Night pod again. A new voice, flat and cold, tasting its first words.
  VICTORY: "What am I?" TRUST: "What will you prepare us to fight?" VICTORY:
  "Everything."

### Act 6 — The window opens

- **f1** — The cataclysm arrives sidelong: monitors in the lab showing a red titan
  wading through boiling sea; then the room shakes, dust falls, lights gutter to red.
  Sirens. (no dialogue)
- **f2** — Red-lit corridor chaos, a collapsed stairwell, Dr. Light's flowchart taped
  to a wall. GYOKUSHO (to Dr. Light, quietly daring): "Ma'am… what happens if we
  evacuate? What happens to — the subject?" DR. LIGHT (smooth): "We take it with us,
  of course." As he walks away, gratitude that is not his own washes over his face.
- **f3** — Dark-only huddle. SHAW: "If we evacuate, we need to kill it." DR. LIGHT:
  "Sabrina shared its mind for weeks and found nothing. We're not going to kill it
  unless it makes us. …Once we're topside, have your people bring out their best.
  All of it."
- **f4** — The pod negotiation, ceiling cracked above the glass. DR. LIGHT: "Good
  evening, Mewtwo." MEWTWO (synthesized, mild): "Good evening, Doctor. Is it a good
  one? Everyone seems rather frightened." DR. LIGHT: "If you leave the pod now, you'll
  likely die before we can repair it." MEWTWO: "How likely is it you'll survive without
  my help? …Then I'll take my chances with the rest of you."
- **f5** — Emergence: rain hammering the plateau; staff applaud as the armored figure
  rises out of the broken ground; the clapping falters and dies. His helmet turns,
  tilts up — rain plinking off the visor. (no dialogue)
- **f6** — The last walk: Mewtwo and Dr. Light strolling the dusk clifftop, guards
  ringed at exactly eight meters, weavile shadowing. MEWTWO: "I wish for my life to
  mean something by my choices, not just my existence. I wish to swim. To fly. To see
  a city, or a forest. Snow." DR. LIGHT (softly): "There are no others like you,
  Mewtwo. There never have been." His tail brushes her stomach — "I'm sorry, Doctor.
  Are you alright?"
- **f7** — Shaw steps closer, jaw set. SHAW: "Have you really given up on yourself?
  If you can't fight for a one percent chance when it's that or death — you'll never
  be what we need you to be." MEWTWO (beat): "Thank you, Mr. Shaw. I'll remember that."
- **f8** — Gyokusho jogs up through thinning rain, beaming. GYOKUSHO: "Groudon's been
  defeated! No new quakes anywhere on the island — and Sabrina just made contact.
  She'll be teleporting here shortly!" Sunset breaks under the clouds and flares off
  Mewtwo's visor. MEWTWO (quiet): "Ah. I suppose I was being pessimistic."

### Act 7 — The escape

- **g1** — THE SNATCH (2 seconds of film, play it at full speed): his tail whips tight
  around Dr. Light mid-sentence and both are instantly airborne, arcing over the cliff
  edge. Shouts; the weavile leaps and is kicked away; a greninja's tongue lashes out —
  he releases the director into the grab — and drops like a stone out of frame.
- **g2** — Free-fall down the cliff face; mid-fall his whole body convulses (the
  kill-switch — potion cut); the sea rushes up. MEWTWO (inner, fragmenting): "—as
  predicted — they cut the potion — now we learn — if I can live without it—" The pain
  visibly fades; his eyes clear. (the hidden healing, revealed)
- **g3** — Wave-skipping: he blurs across the darkening sea, skipping off wavetops like
  a stone, as honchkrow, mandibuzz and a three-headed hydreigon dive after him, dark
  energy blasts detonating the water at his heels. Shaw mounted on his honchkrow:
  "CATCH!"
- **g4** — Underwater: blue-black chaos of bubbles and blood-dim light; four dark
  shapes knife after him; hurled rocks go limp against them. His eyes glow — and the
  sea itself turns: frenzied tentacruel and gyarados swarm his pursuers. He slips into
  a flooded crevice in the island's root. (muffled sound design shot)
- **g5** — loc_cave, pitch black, one blinking suit light, the low-potion beep. He
  wakes on wet sand, clenches a fistful, lets it drip. MEWTWO (inner, slow wonder):
  "I am alive. I am free. I am hungry."
- **g6** — The reckoning, voices in the dark cave. VICTORY (flat): "Doubt, Trust,
  Flourish — they have merged with me. Sentiment is a distraction." MEWTWO (cold
  fury): "Bring them back." A held silence — then the cold voice simply ceases.
  MEWTWO (inner): "I am alone, now. Truly alone."
- **g7** — The suit propped on the sand speaks with Giovanni's voice — a recording,
  triggered, filling the dark. GIOVANNI (V.O., measured): "Hello, Mewtwo. To begin:
  your genetic defect was a lie. We cured it — and kept it as a leash. Sabrina never
  knew. …Any deaths in your escape, I will forgive. Do not become a threat to us, and
  my standing orders are to leave you alone. And Mewtwo — Dr. Fuji is alive and well."
  CU: violet eyes in the dark, unreadable.

### Act 8 — The vaulted sky (coda)

- **h1** — KEY SHOT / title image: he flies out over the black ocean, unsuited, rolls
  slowly onto his back — a moonless night, the stars endless. Tears leave his eyes and
  hang beside his face, caught in the telekinetic field, glinting starlight. MEWTWO
  (inner, soft): "Goodbye, Sabrina. …Not every tear is bitter."
- **h2** — Dawn sea, swimming loose and clumsy and happy beside a pod of curious
  wailmer. MEWTWO (inner): "I have spent a decade wishing to be more human. It seems
  fitting to spend the next one learning to be a pokemon."
- **h3** — SMASH CUT: floating on his back — a flicker below — teeth from nowhere —
  clouds of blood in dark water — he detonates himself and a column of sea into the
  air. Hovering, gasping, he curls forward and lifts what remains of his tail.
  MEWTWO (inner, hollow): "…I've lost my tail." (hold the beat; no music)
- **h4** — FINAL SHOT: night beach. The maimed creature digs himself into the sand
  until only his snout shows, under the same endless stars. MEWTWO (inner, last
  words): "I am alive. I am free." Long hold on the stars. FADE OUT. Title card:
  "THE VAULTED SKY".
- **h5** *(optional stinger, if we want the hook to the rest of the fic)* — Deep
  forest night: hundreds of unown turning in layered rings, "an intricate clock built
  from living aurora"; Mewtwo hovers before it, tiny. MEWTWO (inner): "We must let the
  humans know." CUT TO BLACK.

## Production notes

- **Mouthless dialogue**: every Mewtwo line is telepathy, inner voice, or the suit
  speaker — always prompt "the voice is heard while the creature's face stays
  completely still; no mouth movement." (Proven with the Andalites.)
- **Voice lock pipeline**: cast Mewtwo to one deep text2speech_v2 preset and use TTS
  refs on every clip; separate slightly-processed variant for the synthesized suit
  voice vs. the warmer telepathic voice. Distinct presets for Sabrina, Giovanni,
  Dr. Light, Shaw. Tulpa voices = Mewtwo's preset with direction words (hissed/flat/
  bright) — verify with pitch stats.
- **Species anchor discipline**: mewtwo_pod is the master anchor; every other Mewtwo
  anchor generated from it ("the same creature as the reference image, now wearing…").
  Restate the anatomy checklist (bone-white, violet eyes, three fingers, two horns,
  snout, digitigrade, long purple tail) in EVERY prompt — refs get underweighted.
- **The great ball scene** (d3): capture effect = red light + the man dissolving into
  the ball. Render the ball mid-bounce in the start frame so Seedance animates the
  roll, not the capture physics.
- **Banned-word watch**: avoid "slave(ry)" in prompts (reword: "forever a prisoner");
  keep the rattata bite and tail loss staged as brief, non-gory ("a dark shape, a
  bloom of red in the water" — no wound closeups).
- **On-screen text**: Fuji's sticky note ("You are not alone.") and the title card are
  Nano Banana verbatim-text frames; never ask Seedance to invent text.
- **2-second escape**: g1 wants a single fast take; if Seedance slow-mos it, prompt
  "real-time speed, violent and sudden, no slow motion" and trim hard in the edit.
- Shot count: 52 incl. stinger. Draft pass at 480p fast ≈ 8s × 52 ≈ 620 credits;
  full std master ≈ 1,250 credits. Anchors/frames ≈ 100–150 credits.
