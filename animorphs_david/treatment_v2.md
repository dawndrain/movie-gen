# ANIMORPHS: THE DAVID TRILOGY — treatment v2 (book-faithful)

Rebuilt from the actual books (see books/source_plot.md). Three acts = three
books, narrators' arcs preserved (Marco suspects / Jake fails / Rachel ends it).
No narrator on screen (house rule) — everything is dialogue and staging.

locked cut in STD later. NOTE (Variance lesson): fast mode can't take multiple
--audio refs — concat multi-speaker lines into one mp3 per shot.

## ACT I — THE DISCOVERY (Marco's act: "I don't trust him")

- **d01** (silent, 6s) Cold open. Night construction site across from the mall.
  A flashlight beam; David pries a glowing blue cube out of a broken cement
  block, grins, pockets it. (Reuses v1 a1 staging — still book-accurate.)
- **d02** School hallway, morning. Marco freezes mid-stride: David is carrying
  the cube between classes. MARCO: "Hey. New guy. Where exactly did you get
  that?" DAVID: "Found it. Construction site by the mall. It's for sale — you
  got a hundred bucks?" MARCO: "I've got a dollar thirty-two." DAVID: "Nice
  to meet you. Even nicer to say good-bye."
- **d03** TITLE CARD over the hallway/cube glow: "ANIMORPHS / THE DAVID
  TRILOGY". (v1 a3 asset reusable if we keep construction-site background.)
- **d04** Barn council. AX: "It is the Escafil device. Prince Elfangor carried
  it. It should not exist — and it must not stay with that boy." JAKE: "Then
  we get it back tonight. Quietly. Nobody touches David — he's just a kid who
  found a box."
- **d05** Burger King patio, night. Erek, an ordinary-looking kid, sits down
  uninvited. EREK: "Six world leaders. One resort. Four days from now. And at
  least one of them is already a Controller — we don't know which." JAKE:
  "One crisis at a time, Erek." EREK: "They're the same crisis. If Visser
  Three gets that box the same week he gets the summit... there's no war
  left to fight."
- **d06** (silent, 9s, comedy) The raid fails: osprey-Marco slips through
  David's window — it slams; the tabby cat pounces on him; Rachel's bald
  eagle crashes through the glass; feathers everywhere; DAVID bursts in and
  pumps a BB gun, plinking both birds; Marco drops the cube on the carpet;
  the birds flee over the pool. David, triumphant, in pajamas.
- **d07** School cafeteria. DAVID (leaning in, buddy-buddy): "Somebody online
  wants my box. Serious money. Meeting's today — email goes out on a timer
  before I even get home. You want in? Ten percent for backup." MARCO
  (dead-eyed, hiding panic): "...Sure. Partners."
- **d08** David's house, day. Break-in gone wrong: burglar alarm shrieking,
  Ax's tail sweeping pictures off the wall, hooves skidding on linoleum.
  JAKE-less shot — MARCO: "The computer! Just unplug the — " (downstairs, a
  door: someone's home) " — hide. HIDE."
- **d09** Under David's bed. Marco face-to-face with a rearing cobra in the
  dark; on the desk above, the monitor glows: mail sent. MARCO (whisper):
  "Okay, Spawn. Nice snake. I'm going to touch you now, and you're going to
  let me, because upstairs from now is going to get so much worse." (his hand
  closes on it; the snake goes trance-calm)
- **d10** (silent, 8s) Dusk. A black limousine, two Jeeps and a moving van
  glide onto David's street. Hork-Bajir file across the lawn. Red light in
  the windows. (v1 a5 reusable.)
- **d11** David's bedroom battle. Grizzly-Rachel punches a Hork-Bajir through
  the wall; tiger-Jake roars; VISSER THREE — a scarred Andalite — morphs into
  a hulking purple monster whose cone-tipped arms FIRE across the room like
  rockets, blowing holes in the walls. VISSER THREE: "The box, Andalites.
  Or I redecorate with all of you."
- **d12** Bathroom corner of the battle. DAVID (terrified, in the tub):
  "It's in my backpack! Take it! TAKE IT!" — grizzly-Rachel scoops/shoves him
  through the window as the wall behind them blows out. (cut on the fall)
- **d13** (silent, 7s, dread) Across the street in the bushes: David watches
  his mother and father, escorted — not dragged — into the limousine, calm,
  wrong. The van doors close. His house stands broken and dark.
- **d14** The alley vote. MARCO: "He's seen nothing. He knows nothing. We
  walk away." CASSIE: "He has nowhere to walk TO, Marco." AX: "There is
  another use for the box." JAKE (beat): "We vote." (hands/nods around) JAKE
  (to the waking David): "We're the people who are going to change your
  world, David."
- **d15** Barn initiation. Ax DEMORPHS from human to Andalite in front of
  David (VFX) — David scrambles back into a hay bale. MARCO (flat, no
  comfort): "Yeerks are slugs that take your brain. They have your parents.
  That part's true and it stays true. ...My mom is one. So don't tell me I
  don't know how it feels."
- **d16** 7-Eleven payphone, night. David dials; across the boulevard,
  headlights — Controllers pour out, and among them HIS PARENTS, handing out
  photos of him. DAVID (breaking): "Mom—?" — a rhinoceros gores a parked
  Jeep aside and a gorilla yanks him into the dark. (chaos, discreet)
- **d17** The ceremony. David's palm on the cube between hay bales. JAKE:
  "You'll be the seventh." Blue light climbs his arm. Then the golden eagle
  cage: TOBIAS: "Not the eagle. You don't want to know what golden eagles
  do to red-tails." DAVID (hand already on its shoulder, trance-eyed): "If
  I'm in a war... I want the one that kicks butt."
- **d18** (silent then one line, 10s) David's first morph — feathers, bone,
  wings — and first flight over the night suburbs, ecstatic. Then: he folds,
  stoops, and KILLS A CROW mid-air for nothing. Feathers drift past
  osprey-Marco. DAVID: "Eagle's brain. It just... took over." MARCO (to
  himself, cold): "Sure it did."
- **d19** (silent, 9s) The Blade ship: a shimmer over the coast, a vast black
  hatch swallowing Marine One whole while a hologram of the helicopter flies
  on. (spectacle beat, sets up Act II)
- **d20** Blade ship interior. Five cockroaches scatter between Hork-Bajir
  feet. Above, VISSER THREE (over the unconscious President): "No. Not a
  Controller — too visible, too guarded. I don't need to own the man. I only
  need to BE him, now and then." (his hand closes on the President's wrist —
  acquiring glow) (act-break sting)

## ACT II — THE THREAT (Jake's act: "I let him in")

- **t01** The dunes, morning after. David in borrowed swim trunks and a
  tourist T-shirt. DAVID: "Think about it. Cheetah morph, jewelry store,
  sixty miles an hour. Who'd even chase us?" (silence; everyone looks at
  him) DAVID: "...Kidding. Obviously." JAKE (watching him too long): "Right."
- **t02** (silent, 8s) Seagull recon over the Marriott: agents on roofs, dog
  teams, flags — and a bald man in sunglasses looking UP: an invisible beam
  drops gulls into panicked spirals of pain, one by one. The flock scatters.
- **t03** Barn debrief. JAKE: "My cousin Saddler got hit by a car. It's bad.
  My folks are already down there." (murmured sympathy — CAMERA FINDS DAVID:
  eyes bright, alive, like a lottery winner. He catches himself, arranges
  his face into sorrow.)
- **t04** Holiday Inn room, night. David in a stolen room, TV light. JAKE:
  "You broke a window. You're squatting. We don't steal." DAVID: "You go
  home after this, Jake. You have a BED. How's it end for me? The barn?
  Forever?" JAKE: "Use the power for yourself and you're a danger to all of
  us. And we can't afford you." DAVID (quiet): "Are you threatening me?"
  JAKE (beat): "...Yeah. I guess I am."
- **t05** (silent, 9s, wonder/comedy) Cassie's plan: a dragonfly with five
  fleas aboard hover-darts under a bellman's raised hat brim, rockets down a
  hotel corridor at ceiling height, and slips through an AC vent grate.
- **t06** Inside the fake pillar. A stainless-steel tub of grey sludge —
  a portable Yeerk pool — behind a marble hologram. Jake peers through a
  gap at a man arranging seat cards. JAKE (whisper): "Slashed shoe. That's
  the Visser's man — the protocol chief. The leaders walk RIGHT BEHIND this
  pillar to reach the podium. Grab, infest, and the hologram smiles for the
  cameras."
- **t07** Under the ballroom tables. Alarms, boots, guns. And DAVID —
  grinning, HALFWAY INTO A LION, mane erupting. Jake's bare hand grips the
  mane. JAKE (hissed): "David. Don't. Do. Anything." (the guards pass; the
  lion's lip curls) DAVID: "Hey, Jake. Lion versus tiger. Who wins? ...Lion.
  That's what I think."
- **t08** (silent, 8s) Night raid: birds of prey in a vertical dive, lead
  fishing weights released at speed — thunk, thunk — pool guards drop; a
  cobra rides in on an eagle's talons and doses the third.
- **t09** The trap springs. The "Russian premier" at the podium LAUGHS — and
  Visser Three steps OUT of him like a man stepping out of a costume; the
  glittering banquet crowd vanishes — empty chairs — and a wall of Hork-Bajir
  surrounds them. VISSER THREE: "The real banquet is tomorrow, Andalites.
  Tonight was for YOU."
- **t10** The standoff. DAVID (lion, panicking): "Wait! Don't shoot me! I'll
  demorph — I don't care about these—" (wolf-Cassie CLAMPS his hind leg;
  bear-Rachel cuffs his jaw) — cobra-Marco slides through a Hork-Bajir's
  leg like smoke. MARCO: "They're not real. NONE of them are real." (Ax's
  blade at the Visser's throat; freeze; sirens) VISSER THREE (to David,
  retreating): "Boy. When you're done with these liars — my people are
  watching your old house. Come home."
- **t11** (silent, 8s) The stakeout: Jake huddled in the bed of Cassie's
  dad's pickup, rain on the windshield, 2:30 AM — and a golden eagle slides
  out of the barn's loft door into the dark. In the rafters, a hawk turns
  its head and follows.
- **t12** David's ruined bedroom. A TV dragged in front of the burned bed.
  The eagle on the bedpost. On a wadded sheet: a crumpled red-tailed hawk,
  dead. JAKE (falcon, shattered): "Tobias—" DAVID: "He followed me. Relax,
  Jake, it's not murder. He's a bird. ...There used to be six of you. Now
  there's five. Keep counting."
- **t13** (silent, 10s) Falcon vs eagle in the wrecked room — Jake fights
  un-birdlike, flat on his back, under the bed; rakes David's half-morphed
  face; Hork-Bajir burst in; Ax vaults the pool fence and carries Jake out
  as bladed bodies smash through the fence into the neighbor's pool.
- **t14** Mall roof, night. Tiger and lion circle among the AC units. DAVID:
  "Lion versus tiger, Jake. You never answered." JAKE (letting the tiger's
  growl in): "Let's find out."
- **t15** (silent, 10s) The fight — even, brutal, no gore: mane against
  weight, rolling across gravel — onto the SKYLIGHT. Claws skitter on glass.
  David leaps; Jake can't move; impact — the glass SHATTERS.
- **t16** (silent, 5s) Falling in moonlit shards, twenty feet, teeth locked
  in a throat, down into the dark mall. SMASH CUT TO BLACK. (act break)

## ACT III — THE SOLUTION (Rachel's act: "the terrible thing")

- **s01** Rachel's dark bedroom. A harrier on the sill. AX: "It's David. He
  is a traitor. Tobias is missing and Prince Jake is dying. Rachel — he
  asked for YOU." RACHEL (already morphing owl, voice flat): "I know why."
- **s02** The mall concourse. Under the shattered skylight: a tiger on its
  side in blood. Above, past the bent railing, a lion looks down. DAVID:
  "I don't kill people, Rachel. Birds, sure. Tigers... we'll see." RACHEL
  (screaming up): "I will kill you, David. Look at me. I WILL KILL YOU."
- **s03** (silent→line, 9s) Paramedics, then a vet's van: Cassie's mom over
  the tiger; two flies lift out of the tiger's ear; later — Cassie jabbing
  adrenaline, the tiger staggering up, shrinking, becoming a boy. CASSIE:
  "Seventy more seconds, Jake. Don't you ever do that again."
- **s04** Marco's bedroom, dawn. "Marco" sits up in bed — and bats the
  incoming harrier out of the air with a Louisville Slugger. "MARCO"'s face
  runs like wax into DAVID's grin. DAVID: "One bird down. Tell Rachel I said
  hi." (in the closet behind him, muffled thumping: the real Marco, tied up)
- **s05** (silent, 10s) Owl vs golden eagle over dawn power lines — talons
  sink into Rachel's back — and a red-tailed hawk DROPS out of the clouds
  like judgment, raking David's head. Tobias. Alive. The eagle flees.
- **s06** School cafeteria. "Marco" sits down at their table, too perky —
  behind him, Cassie steers the REAL Marco away from the food line. JAKE:
  "David, I presume." DAVID/"MARCO": "The box. Or Visser Three gets six
  names and six addresses. Your families can join mine. Twenty-four hours."
- **s07** The quad. Rachel spins David around, a cafeteria fork pressed
  under his ear. RACHEL (calm, terrifying): "We have people inside the
  Yeerks. If you sell us, I'll know first. And your parents will never,
  ever come back. Use your imagination." (she walks away; her hand shakes)
- **s08** Hospital corridor. Doctors baffled — the dying cousin is suddenly,
  impossibly perfect. Down the hall, "SADDLER" grins over his shoulder at
  Jake and Rachel. JAKE (one word, ash): "David." RACHEL: "Where's the real
  Saddler, then?" (neither answers; the family celebrates behind glass)
- **s09** (silent, 10s, spectacle) The summit dies: four African elephants
  and two rhinos walk out of the storm surf and simply DEMOLISH the empty
  resort bungalows — flags down, limos crushed, agents scattering. No one
  hurt; nothing left to hold a banquet in.
- **s10** (silent→lines, 12s) Swimming home as dolphins — a killer whale
  hits them like a truck. DAVID (orca): "Five little dolphins. I only count
  FIVE." (a mountain rises under him — humpback-Cassie, vast and calm)
  DAVID (fleeing): "There'll be another time!"
- **s11** Rachel's bathroom, morning. Steam, mirror; she's alone. DAVID
  (from nowhere, tiny, everywhere): "Nice place. I know where you live now.
  All of you. Bring me the box... just you and me, Rachel." (she stands
  very still, then quietly turns off the water) (skin-crawl beat)
- **s12** THE STAGED MEETING. Barn, lantern. In the deep foreground shadow
  under the wall boards: a RATTLESNAKE, coiled, listening. The five perform:
  JAKE (broken): "He's won. We give him the box." MARCO (vicious, at
  Rachel): "And SHE'S the reason. Xena the mighty — beaten by some kid.
  You're a joke." (Rachel lunges; Cassie holds her) CASSIE: "Ax took the box
  APART. The pieces are down a sewer drain at the construction site. You
  want them, you go as a RAT. Nothing bigger fits." (nobody looks at the
  wall)
- **s13** Rafter POV. Below, the rattlesnake pours itself out through the
  gap and is gone. TOBIAS (quiet): "He bought every word. Sidewinding home
  to be Saddler." JAKE (eyes closed): "Then tomorrow we do the worst good
  thing we've ever done."
- **s14** Taco Bell, dusk. "Saddler"-David holds court in a corner booth,
  the team seated in enforced pairs like hostages. DAVID (smearing refried
  beans slowly on Rachel's sleeve): "Not Cassie. HER. Rachel walks me to my
  box. Alone. Say it, Jake — make her." JAKE (dead voice, not meeting her
  eyes): "Do it, Rachel." (Rachel lets a tear fall; the performance)
- **s15** Construction site. Rat-Rachel on the mud — and a RATTLESNAKE
  coils around her, fangs a whisper from her eye. DAVID: "Four birds on the
  wall. All of you — DOWN. Demorph. Now — or I find out if rats die like
  crows."
- **s16** David, human again, scoops four cockroaches off the plywood into
  an empty Pepsi bottle and screws the cap. DAVID: "Visser Three couldn't
  catch you people. I did it with a BOTTLE. Two hours, guys. Enjoy the new
  body." (he grins at the drain) "Ladies first."
- **s17** (silent, 12s, claustrophobic) The pipe: two rats in blind dark,
  whiskers, dripping; a "box piece" dragged clattering; a cut chamber ahead
  — fresh air, wrongness — David panics, LUNGES; teeth in her tail at the
  threshold — Rachel chews through her OWN TAIL — and is through. RACHEL:
  "NOW! NOW! NOW!" — a steel gate GUILLOTINES down. Flashlights blaze.
- **s18** The reveal. Tobias on the pipe, the broken Pepsi bottle glinting
  below; the four, human again, around the cage. MARCO: "The bottle was
  Cassie's idea. The sewer was Cassie's idea. YOU were Cassie's idea — she
  said you'd pick Rachel. You're not even surprising, man." (Rachel opens
  her rat-bitten hand: a LEGO brick) RACHEL: "Here's your box piece."
  (behind them, Cassie stands apart, crying without sound)
- **s19** The two hours. The cage in the flashlight beams. DAVID (small,
  echoing): "Okay. OKAY. I'll leave. You'll never see me — Jake. JAKE.
  You're not GOD!" JAKE: "No. If I were God, Saddler would still be alive."
  AX (even, merciless as a clock): "One hour... and fifty-nine minutes.
  ...Two hours, Prince Jake. It is done."
- **s20** (silent→lines, 11s) The island: an eagle and a harrier take turns
  carrying a rat over the surf to a bare rock far offshore. Rachel sets him
  down. (v1 c10 partially reusable) DAVID (thin, torn by wind): "RACHEL!
  COME BACK! KILL ME! RAAACHEL—" RACHEL (to no one, wet-faced): "I'm
  sorry. It's done." (she doesn't look back)
- **s21** Coda, school hallway, months later. A random kid, mid-story, to
  friends: "—swear to God, this rock way out past the point, dad killed the
  motor and we heard it. A little voice. Crying 'no, no, no'—" (Rachel, at
  her locker, goes still. Tobias on the window ledge outside. She shuts the
  locker, walks on.)
- **s22** (silent, 8s) Wide aerial, dusk: the rock, the surf, a grey speck.
  Gulls. Fade out. (v1 c13 reusable)
