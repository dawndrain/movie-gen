# ANIMORPHS: THE DAVID TRILOGY — film treatment (first pass)

Adapted from K.A. Applegate's *The Discovery / The Threat / The Solution* (Animorphs
#20–22). The Animorphs find the morphing cube in the hands of a stranger, make him
the seventh Animorph, and learn why you don't hand a weapon to someone you don't
know. No narrator; dialogue only (house rule).

Target: ~30 shots × ~8–12s ≈ 5 minutes. 480p std Seedance, Nano Banana anchors/frames,
TTS voice locks per MOVIE_LESSONS.md.

## Cast (anchors)

- **jake** — Jake, 16, the reluctant leader. Tall, broad-shouldered, short brown
  hair, steady tired eyes. Battle morph: siberian tiger.
- **rachel** — Rachel, 16, tall, long blonde hair, fearless and beautiful and a
  little frightening. Battle morph: grizzly bear.
- **cassie** — Cassie, 16, Black, short, kind steady eyes, barn work clothes.
  The moral center; author of the trap. Battle morph: wolf.
- **marco** — Marco, 16, shorter, wiry, shoulder-length dark hair, smirk. The
  one who says the quiet part. Battle morph: gorilla.
- **tobias** — red-tailed hawk (nothlit). Reuse the HB Chronicles hawk anchor.
- **ax** — Aximili-Esgarrouth-Isthill, adolescent Andalite. Same species lock as
  the HB Chronicles Andalites (NO mouth, thought-speak, stalk eyes, tail blade).
- **david** — David, 16, sandy-blond hair, sharp fox-like face, grey hoodie.
  Finds the cube. Becomes the seventh. Becomes the threat. Morphs: golden eagle,
  lion, rat.
- **visser** — Visser Three, the Yeerk in an Andalite body (canonically the same
  Yeerk as Esplin 9466 from the HB film — reuse Roman's voice). Dark, scarred
  Andalite, cold silk.

## Locations (plates)

- **loc_construction** — half-built suburban subdivision at night; mud, plywood
  skeletons, chain-link. Where Elfangor died; where the cube waits. The tiger/lion
  fight returns here (full circle).
- **loc_barn** — Cassie's barn: hay bales, wire animal cages, lantern light,
  gapped plank floors (a rat-sized gap features in c4).
- **loc_hotel** — a glassy resort hotel hosting a world-leaders summit.
- **loc_meadow** — Tobias's meadow and lone oak, golden hour.
- **loc_cove** — rocky cove under cliffs at dawn; tide pools, sea stacks. The trap.
- **loc_island** — a barren wave-washed rock offshore, gulls, grey ocean. The end.
- **loc_bladeship** — Visser Three's blade ship bridge: black steel, red light.

## Voice locks (text2speech_v2 elevenlabs presets — cast after pitch auditions)

- Humans speak with mouths, normal lip-sync.
- Morphed speakers (tiger, lion, bear, rat, hawk, Andalite) use THOUGHT-SPEAK:
  "the voice is heard while the animal's mouth/face never moves."
- Visser Three: Roman preset (continuity with Esplin 9466 in the HB film).
- NO narrator anywhere; only characters on screen speak.

## Shot list

### Act A — The Discovery
- **a1** — (silent) Night. David slips through the construction-site fence,
  flashlight sweeping rubble; the beam finds a glowing blue cube half-buried in
  the mud. He digs it free; blue light on his face.
- **a2** — David's bedroom. DAVID (typing): "Strange blue box, found at a
  construction site. One hundred bucks or best offer. ... Somebody out there wants
  you, little box. I can feel it."
- **a3** — TITLE CARD over the site: "ANIMORPHS / THE DAVID TRILOGY".
- **a4** — Jake's room, Marco's laptop. MARCO: "Jake. Tell me that is not what I
  think it is." JAKE: "The morphing cube. Elfangor's cube." MARCO: "It's on a
  public auction site. If we found it, the Yeerks found it an hour ago."
  JAKE: "Then we go tonight."
- **a5** — (silent, dread) A matte-black Yeerk craft hovers over David's street;
  bladed shapes advance on the house; red dracon light in the windows.
- **v1** — Blade ship. VISSER THREE: "A human child found the Escafil device...
  and offered it for sale. On their internet. Find the boy. Bring me the box.
  And bring me the boy."
- **a6** — Barn, night. DAVID (blanket, shell-shocked): "Those things at my house.
  They took my mom and dad. Tell me what is happening." JAKE: "They're called
  Yeerks. They take people. You can't go home, David. Not ever again."
- **a7** — The cube on a hay bale. JAKE: "Put your hand on it. You'll be one of
  us. The seventh." MARCO (low): "For the record? Bad idea." (David presses his
  hand; blue light climbs his arm.)
- **a8** — (silent) David's first morph: golden feathers ripple up his arms, he
  collapses inward, a golden eagle bursts from the barn loft into the night sky.

### Act B — The Threat
- **b1** — The Gardens, after hours. DAVID (hand on a sleeping lion's flank):
  "Hawks. Wolves. Kid stuff. This is more like it. Hello, king." (Rachel watches,
  uneasy.)
- **b2** — Hotel-overlook briefing. JAKE: "World leaders downstairs, Yeerks in the
  walls. We watch. We report. We do not engage. Nothing happens unless I say it
  happens." DAVID: "You know your problem, Jake? You think you're the boss of me.
  Nobody is the boss of me."
- **b3** — (silent, chaos) A lion where a lion should never be: hotel corridor,
  alarms, shattering glass, guards scattering, the mission burning down.
- **b4** — Barn, the blowup. MARCO: "He broke cover. He blew the mission. He
  almost got us killed. Twice." DAVID: "You get to go home after. To your dad.
  To your bed. I sleep in a barn. I lost everything. Because of you people."
- **b5** — (silent, dread) Tobias's meadow, golden hour: a golden eagle stoops
  like a missile; impact behind the oak; red-tailed feathers drift onto empty grass.
- **b6** — Rachel kneels with feathers in her hand. RACHEL: "He killed him, Jake.
  He killed Tobias. Don't tell me you weren't thinking it too." JAKE: "He wants us
  hunting him angry. We end this my way."
- **b7a** — Construction site, night (full circle). Tiger and lion circle in the
  mud. DAVID: "The famous Jake. Came alone. You should have let me sell the box."
  JAKE: "It ends tonight, David."
- **b7b** — (silent) The fight: tiger and lion crash through plywood and
  scaffolding, dust in the moonlight. No gore.
- **b7c** — The lion pins the tiger at a foundation trench. DAVID: "Say it. Say I
  win." A grizzly explodes through the plywood wall and hurls the lion off;
  it limps into the dark. RACHEL: "Touch him again and there is no morph on Earth
  that will save you."

### Act C — The Solution
- **c1** — Barn. JAKE: "We can't kill him. We can't watch our backs forever. And
  we can't hand him to the Yeerks." CASSIE: "Then we don't do any of those. There
  is a third way. You're all going to hate it. Especially me."
- **c2** — A red-tailed hawk drops onto the rafter. TOBIAS: "Before anyone cries
  at my funeral — the eagle got a wild hawk. Not me. I've been shadowing David for
  two days. I know where he sleeps." RACHEL: "You're alive. Don't you ever do
  that to me again."
- **c3** — (silent) The plan: lantern light, a shoreline map, Cassie whispering,
  Marco marking the cove; outside, a hawk lifts away into the dark.
- **c4** — THE STAGED MEETING. Six around the lantern — and in the foreground, in
  a gap under the floor planks, a brown rat listens, eyes glinting. JAKE (staged,
  a little too loud): "Tomorrow. Dawn. We move the cube to the sea cave at Widows
  Point. Rachel carries it. Alone." MARCO: "Alone? With the one thing the Yeerks
  want more than air?" JAKE: "Alone. After tomorrow, no one will ever find it again."
- **c5** — Rafter POV, the rat slipping out through the wall gap. TOBIAS (quiet):
  "He was here. He heard every word. Hook, line, sinker." JAKE: "Then tomorrow we
  do the worst good thing we've ever done."
- **c6** — Dawn, the cove. Rachel alone on the rocks, blue box under one arm.
  RACHEL (shouting to the wind): "It's just me, David! Just me and the box! I know
  you're watching! Come and take it!"
- **c7** — A lion pads out of the rocks — and the trap springs: bear, gorilla,
  wolf, tiger rise from behind boulders; an Andalite tail blade at the cliff gap;
  a hawk drops to hover. JAKE: "Six against one, David. It's over." DAVID: "The
  meeting. The box. All of it — a play. For me."
- **c8** — Cornered at the cliff, the lion collapses inward — fur greying,
  shrinking — a rat darts into a dead-end crevice. DAVID: "Catch the rat."
- **c9** — THE WAIT. A silent ring of animals around the crevice as the sun
  climbs. AX: "One hour and fifty-nine minutes... Two hours, Prince Jake. It is
  done. He will never morph again. He will never be anything again — except what
  he is." JAKE: "Demorph, everyone. It's over."
- **c10** — THE ISLAND. Rachel sets the rat down on the barren rock. DAVID (small):
  "You can't leave me here. Rachel! RACHEL! Come back! KILL ME!" RACHEL (barely
  holding): "I'm sorry, David. It's done."
- **c12** — Coda, barn, dusk through the slats, six kids and a hawk, silent.
  CASSIE: "We did the only thing we could do." RACHEL: "Then why does it feel
  like this?" JAKE: "Nobody ever mentions David again. That's an order."
- **c13** — (silent) Wide aerial at dusk: the rock island, white surf, a tiny grey
  shape at its center, gulls wheeling. Fade out.
